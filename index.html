General rules

1. No spawncamping, this is defined by bypassing the spawn ramp and killing players as they spawn

2. Cheating is prohitbited, this is defined by injecting dll files into your gmod client

3. Bandits can rob a maximum of 200 gold per act.

4. War must be declared before any house can attack the other, the defenders have 10 minutes to prepare for the attack and build defenses around there castle. 

5. Only engineers can build defenses and ladders to help in sieges. Defenses must be medieval looking props.

6. Engineers can only use their phys gun on their own defenses and ladders. Failure to obey this rule will result in the player losing his class and potential ban depending on the severity.

7. Peasants, farmers, and any merchants must pay taxes to their king or lord if directed to do so by him or his knights. Only the king and knights can do this. You are allowed to defect to other factions if his request is too high.

8. To capture a castle you must capture the village or farm outside the castle, the town, then the keep itself in that order. Once a castle is captured the lord must bow to the victor and you are subject to pay the victor and obey his commands until the next war when you can overthrow them.

9. The Order of Light should side with the less evil side in each war. Whatever they percieve as evil they should fight.

10. You must obey your lord or king at all times.

11. Houses can ally with each other during wars.

12. RDM not allowed (Pagans have exemption)

 

Kingdom of Agatha

The Kingdom of Agatha control all the land and other lords from the start, it is their job to control the land and prevent other lords from warring with each other as well as crime and other forms of banditry. Peace and prosperity is their ultimate goal.

 

Mason Order

The Mason Order resents Agathian control and wishes to rule the Kingdom instead. They should seek to impose their rule on others and enforce their will on all around them. Dominance is their ultimate goal.

 

House Ravenwood

House Ravenwood are rich merchant lords who possess gunpowder technology and makes their fortune selling their goods among the land. They make their profit mostly from war selling goods to both sides. They will often manipulate houses into warring with each other for their own purposes. Wealth is their only care.

 

House Raul

House Raul is the oldest house and is controlled by powerful Vampire lords and knights. They often use magic to assist in their activities which include kidnapping peasants and people for their lord to feed on. They use necromancy to raise undead warriors to defend them. They are hated by most houses as a result. Their ultimate goal is to control the land and enslave the kingdom so they can feed endlessly on their blood.

 

The Pagans (Язычники)

The Pagans are a nomadic people who profit from looting and raiding. They came to this land to seek loot and wealth.

(Язычники - это кочевой народ, который получает прибыль от грабежей и грабежей. Они пришли на эту землю в поисках добычи и богатства.)
